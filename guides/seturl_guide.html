Some people get confused when they try to use ,seturl properly. Some people might remove Inkxbot because it didn't work for them.
To avoid that, there will be a tutorial here on how to use ,seturl correctly for your tournaments.


First off, let's see what your url is. Is it a custom url? A url that's not affilaited?

Below are some guides for affilaited and non-affilaited urls

if you have more questions, feel free to get on the server for help


Guide for non-affilaited urls


Let's say your url is like this.
Since this guide is a simple proccess, all you need to do is to remove http://challonge.com/ from it.

You are left with some letters and numbers from it, this is part of your tournament's id.

You will then use this as your characters for your challonge data.

So then all you have to do is ,seturl (your tournament's id here) and you're all set! Your tournament is registered in the database within 15 seconds!

Guide for affilaited urls


Now let's say your url is like any other Challonge url, BUT it's affilaited with custom affilaited assets.

The only way to extract the tournament's id in this senario is from veiwing the page's source.
This is simple for Chrome browsers to do this thing because it has an option to veiw the pages source.

In the pages source, use Ctrl + F to find a line of text that is called resource_id =
Next to the = in resource_id, you will find some numbers. Those numbers are also your tournament's id.

So like the last guide, you have to do ,seturl (tournament's id here) and boom! That's it! Your tournament is registered in the database!
